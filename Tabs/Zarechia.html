Anquitity

The Kingdom of Zarechia was formed in the 200 BC after the invasion of Flars by the Zark city state. In the terms of the peace treaty it was decided that the leader of Flars would be able to lead Fars relatively independently from Zarkian rule. This was the beginning of the Dual Kingdom of Zark-Farsia.
